Ham and Shrimp Gravy

home

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 6 ounces smoked ham, diced
  • 4 green onions, chopped, white and green parts separated
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely diced
  • ½ cup finely diced celery
  • 1 fresh jalapeño chile pepper, finely diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (Optional)
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ cups chicken broth, cold
  • ¼ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pound small raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pinch salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Directions

Step 1

Melt butter in a deep skillet over medium heat. Add ham; cook until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.

Step 2

Stir in green onion white parts, bell pepper, celery, jalapeño, and garlic; cook and stir until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in Cajun seasoning; cook 1 minute.

Step 3

Stir in flour; cook and stir until flour is no longer gritty, about 3 minutes. Stir in chicken broth and Worcestershire sauce; increase heat to medium-high. Cook until vegetables are soft and liquid is reduced and thick, 10 to 15 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low.

Step 4

Stir in shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp are cooked through, 2 to 3 minutes. Season with salt and cayenne pepper. Garnish with green onion green parts.
